About

Derrick Rose was raised, from the same grimy rough hood as myself, Englewood, Chicago. From high school basketball stardom at Simeon to the NBA, Rose has been the epitome of what we like to call in Chicago, "Gettin'It Out The Mud". His unparalled work ethic, grit, and passion to give it his all day-in and day-out continues to inspire millions across the globe. Personally, his synergy not only consistently drives me, but literally motivated me to write this simple webpage about him some of his greatest accomplishments on and off the court.

On The Court

  • Youngest MVP in NBA History
  • All-NBA First Team Honors (2011), first for Chicago Bulls Since Jordan
  • Won 2019 Moment of The Year with 50-point performance

Off The Court

  • The Rose Scholars: scholarship program that gives back to highschool student
  • Donated $1 million dollars to Chicago's After School Matters program
  • Paid for multiple victims' funerals of Chicago's gun violence
